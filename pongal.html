Ven Pongal

Prep Time: 5 mins----Cook Time: 25 mins----Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice
  • 1/4 cup yellow moong dal
  • 1 tsp whole black pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Cashews for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and moong dal together and keep them aside.
  2. Dry roast the moong dal in a pan for 2-3 minutes until it smells good.
  3. In a pressure cooker, add rice, roasted dal, 4 cups of water and salt.
  4. Cook for 4-5 whistles until both are very soft and well cooked.
  5. Mash the cooked rice and dal together. It should be soft and slightly mushy.
  6. Heat ghee in a small pan. Add cumin seeds and black pepper.
  7. Add ginger, curry leaves and a pinch of hing. Let them fry for 30 seconds.
  8. If using cashews, fry them in the same ghee until golden.
  9. Pour this ghee tadka over the cooked pongal and mix well.
  10. Adjust salt and serve hot with sambar or coconut chutney.
