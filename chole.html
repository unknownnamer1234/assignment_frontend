Chole

Prep Time: 10 mins----Cook Time: 30 mins----Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400g) chickpeas or 1 cup dried (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp chole masala or garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. If using dried chickpeas, boil them with water and salt until soft. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and wait for them to splutter.
  3. Add the chopped onions and fry on medium heat until brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and stir for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add turmeric, coriander powder, red chilli powder and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the boiled chickpeas and stir to coat them in the masala.
  8. Add 1/2 cup water and let it simmer for 10 minutes on low heat.
  9. Add chole masala and mix. Taste and adjust salt.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or bhature.
