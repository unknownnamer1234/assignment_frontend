Egg Curry

Prep Time: 10 mins----Cook Time: 25 mins----Servings: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed or finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp grated coconut or coconut milk
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Curry leaves and mustard seeds for tadka
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes. Peel them and make 2-3 cuts on the sides so the gravy absorbs in.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  3. Add onions and fry on medium heat until they turn dark golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add tomatoes, turmeric, red chilli powder and coriander powder. Mix well.
  6. Cook until tomatoes are soft and oil starts to separate.
  7. Add grated coconut or coconut milk and stir everything together.
  8. Add 1/2 cup water and bring the gravy to a boil.
  9. Add the boiled eggs gently into the gravy.
  10. Add garam masala and salt. Simmer for 5-6 minutes.
  11. Serve hot with rice or appam.
