Upma

Prep Time: 5 mins----Cook Time: 20 mins----Servings: 2-3

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chilli, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans) - optional
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander and lemon juice for garnish

Steps

  1. Dry roast the semolina in a pan on low heat for 3-4 minutes until it smells nutty. Keep it aside.
  2. Heat oil in the same pan. Add mustard seeds and chana dal.
  3. When mustard seeds pop, add curry leaves and green chilli.
  4. Add onions and fry until they turn transparent.
  5. Add vegetables if using and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Pour in 2 cups of water and add salt. Bring it to a boil.
  7. Slowly add the roasted semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  8. Stir quickly and mix well so there are no dry bits.
  9. Cover the pan and cook on low heat for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Squeeze lemon juice on top and garnish with coriander.
  11. Serve hot as a breakfast or snack.
