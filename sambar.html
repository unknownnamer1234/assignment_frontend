Sambar

Prep Time: 15 mins----Cook Time: 30 mins----Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup toor dal
  • 1 small drumstick, cut into pieces (or any vegetable you like)
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 small ball of tamarind (soaked in warm water)
  • 2 tsp sambar powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • For tadka: 1 tsp mustard seeds, 1 dry red chilli, curry leaves, 1 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Wash and boil toor dal with turmeric until it is fully cooked and soft.
  2. Mash the dal well and keep it aside.
  3. Squeeze the soaked tamarind to get tamarind water. Discard the pulp.
  4. In a pot, add the tamarind water, tomato, onion and vegetables.
  5. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes until vegetables are half cooked.
  6. Add sambar powder and salt. Stir well.
  7. Add the mashed dal and mix everything together.
  8. Add water to adjust thickness and bring it to a boil.
  9. Simmer for 8-10 minutes on low heat.
  10. For tadka: heat oil, add mustard seeds. When they pop, add red chilli and curry leaves.
  11. Pour the tadka into the sambar and serve hot with rice or idli.
