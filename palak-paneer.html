Palak Paneer

Prep Time: 15 mins----Cook Time: 25 mins----Servings: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 2 bunches of fresh palak
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the spinach and boil it in water for 3 minutes. Drain and let it cool.
  2. Blend the boiled spinach into a smooth puree and keep it aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped onions and fry until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  7. Add turmeric and salt, mix well.
  8. Pour in the spinach puree and stir everything together.
  9. Let it cook on medium heat for 5-6 minutes.
  10. Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Mix gently.
  11. Cook for 3-4 more minutes. Add cream if you like and serve hot.
