Aloo Gobi

Prep Time: 10 mins----Cook Time: 25 mins----Servings: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and fry until light golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato, turmeric, red chilli powder, coriander powder and salt.
  5. Cook the masala until the tomato becomes soft and oil separates.
  6. Add the potato cubes and stir to coat with the masala.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 8 minutes, stirring once in between.
  8. Add the cauliflower florets and mix gently.
  9. Cover again and cook for another 10 minutes on low heat.
  10. Check if both vegetables are cooked through. Adjust salt if needed.
  11. Garnish with coriander and serve with chapati or rice.
