Dal Tadka

Prep Time: 5 mins----ook Time: 25 mins----Servings: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow moong or toor dal
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 4-5 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 dry red chilli
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Wash the dal and boil it with 2.5 cups water, turmeric and a pinch of salt.
  2. Cook until the dal is completely soft. Keep it aside.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a separate pan for the tadka.
  4. Add cumin seeds and dry red chilli. Let them splutter.
  5. Add sliced garlic and fry until it turns golden.
  6. Add onions and cook until they are light brown.
  7. Add tomato and red chilli powder. Cook until tomato is soft.
  8. Pour the cooked dal into this tadka pan and stir well.
  9. Add more water if it is too thick. Add salt and mix.
  10. Simmer for 5 minutes on low heat.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
